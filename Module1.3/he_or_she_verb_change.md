## He / She + Verb change 

    এটা English speaking-এর সবচেয়ে important rule 🔥

    🔹 Rule:

        👉 যখন Subject = He / She / It
        👉 তখন Verb-এ "s" বা "es" যোগ হয়

    🔹 Structure:

        👉 He/She + Verb(s/es) + Object

            🔹 Example দিয়ে বুঝি
            Example 1:

                He eats rice

                👉 He = সে (ছেলে)
                👉 eat → eats (s যোগ হয়েছে)
                👉 rice = ভাত

            Example 2:

                She reads a book

                👉 She = সে (মেয়ে)
                👉 read → reads
                👉 a book = একটি বই

            Example 3:

                He plays football

                👉 play → plays

    ⚠️ Important Rule

        👉 I / You → verb change হয় না
        👉 He / She → verb + s/es
